The finance team changed the invoice review after two vendors were paid late in March. A coordinator now checks the shared inbox each morning and assigns every message to one owner.

Most delays came from missing purchase-order numbers, not from approval disputes. Forms failed. The team added a required field to the intake form and sent vendors a one-page guide.

The first month was uneven. Three invoices still needed manual follow-up, but the average review time fell from six days to three, allowing the controller to close the month earlier.

Work remains. The next review will focus on duplicate vendor records, procurement handoffs, and the old supplier accounts that still point to retired approvers.
