The committee reviewed the pilot and found three problems that slowed adoption.

- **Onboarding**: New members received a long packet but no clear first task, so several waited for direction.
- **Scheduling**: Meetings moved between mornings and evenings, which made attendance difficult for hourly workers.
- **Reporting**: Progress notes lived in separate folders, so the coordinator rebuilt the same update twice.
- **Ownership**: No one knew who could approve a small budget change between meetings.

The next version should assign one coordinator, publish a fixed meeting time, and replace the packet with a short checklist.
