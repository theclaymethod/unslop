The ferry schedule changed after the west dock repair reduced the number of usable slips. Morning boats now leave twelve minutes earlier, and the last evening crossing runs only on weekdays.

Commuters adjusted fastest when the port posted paper notices beside the ticket machines. The online alert helped less because several regular riders buy passes in person.

The harbor office added a temporary bench and a covered sign near the backup line. Those small changes made the wait easier during the first week of the repair.

By Friday, the crew had replaced the damaged fenders and painted the lane markers for the temporary boarding area.
